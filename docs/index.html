Application Settings

Powerful Features

🔒

100% Offline & Private

Runs completely offline using a built-in llama-completion sidecar. Your text never leaves your device.

⚡

Global Hotkey

Quickly select any text, press Ctrl+Alt+P (or Cmd+Alt+P on Mac), and watch it load instantly.

📋

Auto-Paste Back

Seamlessly writes polished text to your clipboard and injects it back into your active window.

🛡️

Clipboard Safety

Backs up and restores your pre-existing clipboard contents automatically to prevent pollution.

☁️

Cloud Provider Support

Optional support for OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or Groq if you prefer online models.

🚀

Cross-Platform

Available and fully compatible with Windows and macOS (Apple Silicon and Intel).
